famous arts from the past

The Mona Lisa is one of the most famous paintings in the world, known for its enigmatic smile and captivating gaze. Painted by Leonardo da Vinci, it's believed to have been started in 1503 or 1504 and completed around 1517, although there's debate among scholars about the exact timeline.

Vincent van Gogh's "Starry Night" is an iconic painting created in 1889 during the artist's stay at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum in Saint-Rémy-de-Provence, France. It's one of his most recognized and celebrated works, renowned for its swirling, expressive style and vibrant use of color.
